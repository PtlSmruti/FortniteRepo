WELCOME TO GAME MODE!

The following are a few instructions.

Press WASD to move the player

To win the game, defeat the enemies and collect as many coins they leave behind.

Pick up the weapons by moving over it and use SPACE to switch between the weapons.

To use an AXE press ENTER. The axe can only cut down trees and it cannot harm the enemies.



To exit the game, press the EXIT button.

And Remember to have FUN!
